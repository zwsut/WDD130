Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to grow our cutomer base. The site will be another place for potential customers to receive information about our rafting services, as well as a place to book trips. It will also serve to give customers information to connect to our customer service representatives through phone and email.

Audience

Our audience is younger to middle age adults who are physically active adventure-seekers. They will generally be financially stable and able to spend for the thrill-seeking activities our company provides. We seek to provide an easy introduction point to white water rafting by providing all the gear, training, and team leaders so no previous experience is required for a safe experience. This site will be accessed through all types of devices, but we anticipate mobile users will be the majority.
